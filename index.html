About Gary

Gary is a digital artist who blends bright expressionism with sleek futuristic themes. Over the last eight years, Gary has crafted visuals for indie games, editorial pieces, and personal series that celebrate the contrast between organic movement and digital precision. When not building new sketches, Gary writes about the tools, techniques, and ideas that shape the creative process.
